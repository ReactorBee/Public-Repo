2. Information We Do NOT Collect

ZerosX is a simple, offline-first game that prioritizes your privacy:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers.
  • No Location Data: We do not access or collect your device's location information.
  • No Device Information: We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or device specifications.
  • No Usage Analytics: We do not track how you play the game, your scores, or your gaming patterns.
  • No Network Data: The game works entirely offline and does not transmit data over the internet.

3. Local Data Storage

ZerosX may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Game Settings: Your preferred symbol (X or O) and difficulty level
  • Score History: Wins, losses, and draws (stored locally only)
  • Game State: Current game progress (if you close and reopen the app)

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

4. Third-Party Services

ZerosX does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The game is completely self-contained.

5. Children's Privacy

ZerosX is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from users of any age. Our game is designed to be safe and appropriate for children.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there are no data security concerns related to external data storage. All game data remains securely on your device.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there are no data processing activities to opt out of. You have complete control over your local game data and can:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Reset game statistics through the app's restart function
  • Change game settings at any time

8. App Permissions

ZerosX requests minimal permissions:

  • Internet Access: Not required - the game works offline
  • Storage: Only for local game data storage
  • Camera/Microphone: Not used
  • Location: Not accessed
